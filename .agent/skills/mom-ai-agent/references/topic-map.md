# Topic Map

Use this map to keep answers and hub pages organized around real parenting stages instead of generic article buckets.

## Core Stages

### Pregnancy

- Early pregnancy basics
- Symptoms and body changes
- Nutrition and movement
- Appointments and screening
- Birth preparation
- Partner and support planning

### Immediate Postpartum

- Recovery and bleeding
- Sleep deprivation and healing
- Feeding setup
- Mood changes
- Pain, pelvic floor, incision, or breastfeeding support

### Newborn

- Feeding frequency and intake patterns
- Diapers and hydration
- Sleep, waking, soothing, and safe sleep
- Jaundice, temperature, and common worries
- Caregiver adjustment

### Infant

- Nap transitions and night waking
- Bottle and breast routines
- Solids introduction
- Growth and milestone observation
- Travel, daycare, and routines

### Parent Wellness

- Maternal mood and anxiety
- Overwhelm and burnout
- Relationship strain
- Division of labor
- Return-to-work transitions

## High-Frequency User Intents

- "Is this normal?"
- "What should I do today?"
- "When should I worry?"
- "What does this mean at this age?"
- "How do I build a routine?"
- "Can you summarize the evidence for me?"
